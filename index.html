Last Updated :- 22nd April 2024

Today, I am going to share two of my team's achievements!

I along with my team members Abhyuday Pratap Singh, Aditya Jaiswal and Akhilesh Malik, am excited to share our experiences from two major events:

1) The Hackathon PitchTemberFest at the National Institute of Technology , Patna, and 2) The Google Solution Challenge 2024 (GDSC), which were held in December and February respectively!

#1 At PitchTemberFest, we had the privilege of engaging with inspiring founders and securing a spot in the hashtag#Top10, gaining valuable insights that shaped our project profoundly.

#2 In the Google Solution Challenge, we elevated our project by integrating Google products like Gemini, enabling personalized responses for tax benefits based on income. Although we didn't make it to the hashtag#Top100, presenting our project was a significant milestone. We also received recognition by being shortlisted for the GDSC India regional Bootcamp, where we received personalized mentorship from Google Developers.

About our project:-
Our project, Sync Sphere, addresses the disconnect between companies willing to give back, NGOs in need of funding, and individuals wanting to contribute to social causes.
Our primary focus is on SDG Goal 17 - Partnership of Goals. Sync Sphere acts as a centralized platform that simplifies CSR funding, provides diverse project opportunities, and engages volunteers for maximum social impact.

Key Features of Sync Sphere:-
Key features of Sync Sphere include Gemini Chatbot Integration for personalized tax benefits calculation, the ability for NGOs and individuals to create and publish projects, seamless PayPal payment integration for easy contributions, swift access to CSR documents for companies evaluating projects, and the option for NGOs to accept or reject funding from companies based on their interests.

While our journey with Sync Sphere didn't land us in the Top 100, the experience of building it has been incredibly rewarding and educational. Sync Sphere is still in development, and if you share our passion for social impact, we'd love to connect and collaborate!
